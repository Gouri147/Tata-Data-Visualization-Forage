#  Tata Data Visualization Virtual Internship (Forage)

##  Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Tata Forage Virtual Internship, where I worked as a data analyst for an online retail company. The objective was to analyze business performance and provide insights to support decision-making for the CEO and CMO.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends and seasonality
- Identify top-performing countries and products
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Provide data-driven recommendations for business expansion

---

##  Data Cleaning
- Removed records with Quantity < 1
- Removed records with Unit Price < 0
- Handled missing Customer IDs
- Created Revenue column (Quantity × Unit Price)

---

##  Dashboard Insights

### 1. Revenue Trend (2011)
- Clear seasonal patterns observed
- Helps in forecasting and planning

### 2. Country Performance
- Top countries: Eire (~124.72K), Netherlands (~104.23K), Germany (~95.39K)
- Low-performing countries: Saudi Arabia, USA, Bahrain
- Revenue concentrated in few regions

### 3. Product Analysis
- Top product: POST (~27,203.94)
- Other strong product: M (~19,492)
- Some products have negligible sales

### 4. Customer Analysis
- Customer ID 1300 has highest purchase frequency
- Revenue driven by a small group of high-value customers

---

##  Key Recommendations
- Expand in high-performing countries
- Focus on customer retention strategies
- Optimize low-performing products
- Use seasonal trends for planning
